Well Drilling on the Lange Farm

This photograph captures a pivotal moment of modernization on a rural American farm: the drilling of a new well on the Lange Farm, north of Ebenezer. The scene is a fascinating snapshot of a transitional era, featuring a portable cable-tool drilling rig powered by a horse-drawn chassis on the left, while an early automobile, likely a Model T, is parked near the modest farmhouse on the right. For a farm family, a new well meant a more reliable and safer source of water, securing the farm's viability and representing a significant improvement in their quality of life.
